1. Information We Collect

At PetQuirky, we believe in radical transparency. When you browse our collection of bespoke pet furniture or artisan treats, we collect information that helps us personalize your "nesting" experience. This includes personal identifiers like your name, email address, and shipping details provided during checkout.

We also automatically collect technical data through your interaction with our site, such as your IP address, browser type, and the delightful path you take through our editorial stories. This allows us to ensure our "tactile digital space" remains functional and fast for every visitor.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to enrich the PetQuirky community. Primarily, we use it to fulfill your orders, manage your account, and keep you updated on the latest sustainable pet innovations.

"We treat your data like we treat our pets—with the utmost care, protection, and respect for their boundaries."

We may also use your behavior patterns to improve our UI/UX, ensuring that our asymmetrical, editorial layouts remain as intuitive as they are beautiful. Your feedback and usage statistics are the heartbeat of our independent boutique operations.

3. Third-Party Sharing

PetQuirky does not sell your data to corporate conglomerates. We only share essential information with our trusted partners who make our service possible: secure payment processors, eco-conscious shipping couriers, and the technical infrastructure that hosts our digital home. These partners are legally bound to protect your information as strictly as we do.

4. Your Rights (GDPR)

As a European-inspired boutique, we fully adhere to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). You have the following absolute rights regarding your personal data:

  • check_circle
    Right of Access: Request a full transcript of every piece of data we hold about you.
  • check_circle
    Right to Erasure: Request that we delete all your records from our systems at any time.
  • check_circle
    Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

5. Cookies

We use "cookies" (the digital kind, though we prefer the artisan variety) to remember your preferences and cart items. These small files are stored on your device to help us recognize you when you return to PetQuirky. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings, though some of our more curated features may lose their magic.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, our tiny team is here to help. Reach out to our Privacy Officer directly.
