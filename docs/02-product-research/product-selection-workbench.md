> Role owner: Product Selection Lead

## Current Selection Question

Which products should PetQuirky choose for the first serious validation batch?

## Current Product Strategy

PetQuirky uses a dual-track product strategy:

- **Cat/Dog Traffic Products**: use larger audience and bestseller potential to drive TikTok traffic, trust, first clicks, and first orders.
- **Exotic Pet Profit Products**: use niche expertise, lower competition, and stronger care needs to create margin, differentiation, and SEO depth.

Default first validation batch:

- 2-3 cat/dog traffic products.
- 2-3 exotic pet profit products.
- 1 bundle or AOV booster opportunity.

## Fast Reject Checklist

Mark any candidate that triggers these:

- [ ] IP / infringement / platform risk
- [ ] Weak gross margin after shipping and fees
- [ ] Heavy or bulky shipping risk
- [ ] Fragile or high return risk
- [ ] Complex installation or unsafe pet usage risk
- [ ] Supplier instability
- [ ] Cannot support 3+ TikTok content angles
- [ ] Weak product page story
